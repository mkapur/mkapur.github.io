Sustainability Management Science

You can find an up-to-date list of my peer-reviewed publications on Google Scholar.

Because much of my work directly informs the management of industrial fisheries in the United States and international waters, a large body of my research is contained in grey literature: technical memoranda and formal stock assessment reports submitted to regional fishery management organizations. A list of these publications, and links (where available), is provided below.
